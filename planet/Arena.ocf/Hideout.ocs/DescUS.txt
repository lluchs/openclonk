 Hideout

Protect your hideout from the opposing team and try to capture the enemy flag.

Goal: Steal your enemies' flag and bring it to your base. 

- The gates are destructible and open automatically for friendly clonks.
- Chests inside the hideouts contain basic weapons.
- Chests outside the hideouts contain more advanced weaponry.
- The central chest contains only gems and other special stuff.
- Throw gems! They activate on impact.
- Killed enemies leave a healing gem behind. Use it to slowly heal your clonk.
- Your flag must be in your base for you to score.
